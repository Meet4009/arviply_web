Our Innovation

Arvy Ply

01

Dryer

100% VPT treated veneers go throught 12-stage drying process to ensure optimum drying and even moisture distribution.

02

Composer

These veneers are composed using imported core and L-type panel composer to make gapless 8x4 sheets with alternate grain structure called core and panels

03

Glue Spreader

These core and panels are glued using 100% BWP grade phenolic resins with added preservatives to ensure optimum bonding and protection against termites borers and fires

04

Pre-Presser

The ply is cold pressed to ensure even glue distribution across the sheet to avoid any delamination issues

05

Hot Press

The ply is then pressed under high temperature to ensure tat the phenolic resin cures and desired bonding is achieved.

06

Cutting DD Saw

The matte ply is then trimmed on all four sides to achieve the desired length and width

07

Calibrator

Tjis matte ply is then calibrated using a heavy duty calibrator to achieve uniform thickness across the sheet

08

Puttifilling Line

Any minor holes which are natural to the veneer are carefully filled using skilled workmanship

09

Glue Spreader

100% imported gurjan veneer is glued on both sides of the matte ply with 100% melamine resin with preservatives that imparts stength and sleekness to the plywood

10

Pre-Press

Pre-press ensures even distribution of the resin and optimum bonding

11

Hot Press

This is the final stage of the 4 press technology where the final ply is pressed at high temperatures

12

Cutting DD Saw

Any excess material is trimmed as a part of the final sizing of the plywood to achive the desired size

13

Sander

The plywood undergoes final sanding to ensure that uniform thicknessm is achieved

14

Dipper

The plywood surface is treated with preservatives for protection against termites and bores and fire.

15

Final Step

The Final touch that makes the ply ready to use
